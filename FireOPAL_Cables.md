The FireOPAL cables provide power the computer inside the FireOPAL
enclosure and connect the PCB to the computer and the camera.

## DC Connector to Input of Relay

### Items required

- 1x 2 row 8 way Molex Minifit Jr. Connector Housing
- 2x 18-24 AWG Molex Minifit Jr. Crimp Contact Female
- 1x length 185mm Yellow 18 AWG wire
- 1x length 185mm Black 18 AWG wire
- 1x 02x05 IDC Connector Housing
- 10x Harwin M20 Female Crimp contacts
- 1x length 155mm purple **22?** AWG wire
- 1x **22-28?** AWG Molex Minifit Jr. Crimp Contact Female

### Instructions

Strip one end of yellow and black wires and crimp 18-24 AWG Molex
Minifit Jr. Contacts on one end. Once crimped, insert two wires into 2
row 8 way Molex Minifit Jr. connector Housing as pictured in the image
below.

pic: FireOPAL_Cables/02x04_Molex1.jpg

With the connector horizontal, with the side where contacts are inserted
facing the user and the clip on top, the crimped wires are inserted on
the left-hand side of the connector. Yellow wire is inserted in the top
row and the black wire is inserted in the same, left-most column, on the
bottom row.

Strip the purple wire and crimp **18-24?** AWG Molex Minifit Jr. Contact
on one end. Insert into bottom right corner of the Molex Minifit Jr.
Connector Housing oriented as described previously (Contact insert side
facing user and clip facing up).

pic:FireOPAL_Cables/02x04_Molex2_Purple.jpg

Strip other end of purple wire and crimp M20 22-28 AWG Harwin Contacts
to wire. Take M20 02x05 IDC Connector Housing and insert the crimped
wire into contact number 1 (marked by arrow on black housing). A
connector housing with the wire inserted in the proper position is
pictured below. Note the position marker in the image.

pic: FireOPAL_Cables/02x05_IDC_Purple.jpg

For the same connector, take one contact, crimp it and fill the end
opposite to the where the cable would sit with solder. This contact is
inserted in the position opposite to position 1 (where purple wire is
inserted) on the same row. The contact ensures the right orientation of
the connector when it is plugged in the computer motherboard. Crimp
contacts without any cables inserted to fill the rest of the positions
in the connector. A finalised connector is illustrated in the image
below.

pic: FireOPAL_Cables/02x05_IDC_2.jpg

Strip other ends of black and yellow wires and crimp 3mm - Red - ring
terminals. Connect terminals to the smaller solid state relay terminals
facing away from the plate bend. The terminal locations are highlighted
in the image below.

pic: FireOPAL_Cables/18awg_relay_cables.svg

## Relay OUT to ceramic block connector - 16AWG

Components required:

- 2x length yellow 16 AWG wire 105mm long
- 1x blue fork terminal
- 1x blue ring terminal

Instructions:

Trim one end of both wires. Slightly twist together, insert into ring
terminal and crimp. Similarly, strip other ends of wires and slightly
twist wires. Take wires and insert in fork terminal and crimp. Pictured
is a final cable.

## 6 Pin DC Power connector

### Components required:

Original 6 pin Molex Minifit Jr. Connector - black colour

- 6x 16 or 18-24AWG Molex Minifit Jr. Crimp Contact Female
- 2x 16 AWG yellow wire **xx mm**

<!-- -->

- 2x 16 AWG black wire **xx mm**
- 1x blue ring terminal 5mm
- 1x blue fork terminal 5mm

### Instructions:

Yellow wires:

1.  Strip one end of both yellow wire lengths and insert both ends into
    blue ring terminal and crimp.
2.  Strip other end of both yellow wire lengths and crimp Molex Minifit
    Jr. contacts.
3.  Insert crimped contacts into 2 row 4 way Molex Minifit Jr. Connector
    Housing in the row closest to the fixing clip skipping the middle
    position.
4.  Connect ring terminal to left-most output of Solid state relay.

Black Wires:

1.  Strip one end of both black wire lengths and insert both ends into
    blue fork terminal and crimp.
2.  Strip other end of both black wire lengths and crimp Molex Minifit
    Jr. contacts.
3.  Insert crimped contacts into 2 row 4 way Molex Minifit Jr. Connector
    Housing in the row away from the fixing clip skipping the middle
    position.
4.  Connect fork terminal to left-most position of ceramic connector.
