Very straight forward to use instrument:

- Powered by 10x AA batteries, already in. Check total voltage of
  battery brick \>14V, otherwise replace.
- Plug in GPS antenna.
- Power ON, wait for GPS (top right ¥ symbols are indicative of GPS
  lock).
- Start recording.
- After pressing the stop button, wait for a few seconds for data access
  to SD card to stop before turning off.
