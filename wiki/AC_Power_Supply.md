The power supply recommended for AC powered installations where the
observatory is located close to the AC power supply is the [Meanwell

for any voltage drop in the long cable runs. (If no heaters are present,

the sunlight and abrasion resistant AlphaWire EcoFlex PUR 80045 SL005
