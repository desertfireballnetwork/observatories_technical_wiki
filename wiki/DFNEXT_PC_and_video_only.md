First connect the main DFNEXT camera (DFNEXT040), and give it 5 minutes
after boot.

Then connect:

- video camera straight to USB3 port
- HDMI cable to portable screen
- ethernet cable to outside port of the main DFNEXT on bottom port.
- keyboard and mouse in grey USB leads.
- finally, power straight on PC board.

To focus, follow instructions
[here](Focusing_the_video_lens "wikilink").

Point it East (270 deg azimuth), and about 55 degrees up.

Or use JAXA app (aim is to point it at the 90-100km point on the
trajectory.

If possible, align the long edge of the sensor with the trajectory to
capture more of it.
