# Observatory Hardware

1.  Hardware overview
    (https://dfn.d-f-n.org//mediawiki/index.php?title=DFNEXT)
    1.  Components
    2.  Indicator LEDs
2.  What needs to work to take pictures
3.  How to avoid damaging the observatory hardware
    1.  Take care when insert
    2.  Use pliers to avoid damaging the LC shutter connector
    3.  Be careful with UFL connectors and consider using pliers
    4.  Support Molex Mini-Fit Jr connectors from behind when plugging
        in
4.  Take care when servicing the observatory, damage to cables and
    connectors will reduce reliability
5.  Common maintenance tasks
    1.  Changing drives
    2.  Formatting the CF card
    3.  Cleaning and recoating the lenses
    4.  Changing the blower duct (use flexible weatherproof silicone
        sealant, never use a hard glue)
    5.  Changing the modem or SIM card
    6.  Changing the DSLR or lens
    7.  Changing the PCB
    8.  Changing the USB WiFi adaptor (we have one case where a WiFi
        adaptor stopped the camera from booting)
